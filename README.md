## Campaign Overview

**Strategy:** Hero → Bite-Sized Ecosystem  
One anchor video (the origin post) becomes a network of short vertical clips. Every clip drives traffic back to the original.

**Series:** Umma 420 Event — backyard cookout vibe, jazz / hip-hop / house  
**Rollout:** 1 clip every other day over 2 weeks. Umma posts other content between drops.

## Linking Strategy

| Method | How it works |
|---|---|
| Same audio | Extract audio from origin → use on all clips. Tap audio → lands on original. |
| Remix | Tap original → Remix. Shows "Remix from…" on each clip. |
| Add Yours | Add sticker to origin. Respond to own sticker with clips. Stacks under one thread. |

---

## Caption Rules (All Clips)

Every short clip caption reinforces the funnel:
- "Full video on my page"
- "Watch the full set"
- "This was just one moment…"

## Notes

- Instagram has no official backlink system for your own posts. Real linking power comes from audio, remix, and viewer curiosity → profile click.
- Widescreen origin video reads premium and stands out in a vertical-first feed.
- Dropping clips 1–2 hours after the origin signals to the algorithm that the content has layers — boosts session time across posts.
- Dun Deal / "Stoner" connection is the trap entry point into what is otherwise a jazz/house/hip-hop event.
