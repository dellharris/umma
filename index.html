Step 1

Post the Origin Video First (16:9)

  • Upload the full widescreen video as the first post
  • Write a strong hook caption — this is what everything points back to
  • Widescreen reads premium and stands out in a vertical-first feed
  • Post at peak time for your audience
Step 2

Cut Short Vertical Clips (9:16)

  • 5–10 sec drop moment — hook with raw energy
  • 5–15 sec reaction/funny clip — built for shares
  • 5–10 sec strong transition — visual intrigue
  • 10–15 sec best line — emotional pull

These are traffic drivers. Their only job is to send people to the full video.

Step 3

Link Every Clip Back to the Original

Option A

Same Audio

Extract audio from origin and use it on every clip. People tap the sound and land on the original. Hidden link network.

Option B

Remix

Tap the original post and Remix it. Creates a visible "Remix from..." label on each clip.

Option C

Add Yours

Add an "Add Yours" sticker to the origin. Respond to your own sticker with each clip. Everything stacks under one thread.

Step 4

Caption Strategy

Every short clip caption should push viewers toward the full video.

"Full video on my page"
"Watch the full set"
"This was just one moment..."
"You need to see how this starts"

Train viewers to go deeper — not just scroll past.

Advanced Play

Posting Order

Drop origin video at peak time

Strong hook caption. Let it breathe.

Wait 1–2 hours

Let the algorithm index the original post.

Start dropping clips one by one

Signals to IG that this content has layers. Boosts session time across posts.
